About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a passionate stickman animator dedicated to bringing simple characters to life through creative and engaging animations.

With years of experience in animation, I specialize in creating dynamic stickman sequences that tell stories, convey emotions, and entertain audiences of all ages.

My work combines traditional animation principles with modern techniques to create smooth, expressive, and memorable animated content.
